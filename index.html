Benni's Zah

Benni's Zah is a traditional, family owned Italian cuisine-focused restaurant. Our restaurant was established almost 29 years ago. Since it's opening, it has remained one of the top favourites on the Limerick scene. Located in the very heart of town, Benni's is a meeting place for people from all walks of life. At Benni's we cater for all varied tastes... not just for the Italian blood in us! We also have an excellent choice of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Come and check us out!
